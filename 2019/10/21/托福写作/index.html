Living in the age of information explosion.people are constantly exposed to all kinds of news from newspaper,TV programs and the internet.Then the question arises: is it necessary for us to know things that do not affect our lives?It seems that it is a waste of time to care about what’s happening in the world especially when they are not related to us.However,as far as I am concerned,I believe that it is better for us to play close attention to world news.
To begin with,knowing about things happening in the world makes people from different cultures gain a deeper understanding of each other.With the advancement of technology, we are now practically living in a global village,As a member of this village,we should be more concerned with the general trend of its affairs.Only in this way can we develop a harmonious relationship with other members in this village and be generous to each other.For example, when people from different countries are in dispute,it can be more easily setted if they are acquainted with each other’s cultural background.Therefore,as citizens of the world,we should learn what’s happening outside our little community.
In addition,by learning about what’s going on around the globe,we can keep up with the fast pace of the world.As a modern person,one has to absorb as much information as possible no matter it is a peice of local or international news.The information will keep him from being left behind by the progress of society.Fo example,when the American astrinauts set their feet on the moon,it is more a milestone in human history than a monumental moment for United States.When people in other countries knew this,they were aware of the huge progress that the whole world has made and would be inspired to make another heart-stirring breakthrough.Therefore,would news will keep us updated on our latest improvements and boost our confidence about what we can do.
Last but not least,things that seem irrelevant to our daily lives may turn out to be quite influential.It may first appear to people that issues that plague other countries have nothing to do with their life.However,we have encountered many crises which originate from other countries.Take Japanese nuclear leak as an example: since people got informed of this information,they would not catch the poisonous sea food to eat.If left neglected by people in China,this event may cause great damage to prople’s life.It is of critical importance to learn international affairs.
In conclusion,as cosmopolites,in order to get along with prople from other countries,to take precautions when something bad happens in order areas of the world and to keep pace with the times,we should definitely read or watch world news.
